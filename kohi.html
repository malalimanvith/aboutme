Coffee


I used to hate the taste of coffee. It was always too bitter, too watery, too hot, and generally a hassle. I refused to be defeated by a little brown bean, so I started experimenting with new ways to make coffee until I landed on something that worked, and I fell in love with the tinkering process. Of course, having parents that love coffee helps quite a bit!
