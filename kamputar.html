Computers


Computers are really fascinating once you think about it. A bunch of invisible charges and wires somehow capable of playing video games? Really, how did humanity create those out of a bunch of silicon wafers? My journey with computers started when I was 7, taking apart, fixing and then breaking my dad's old HP Pavilion. After that, my interest snowballed until I was in cybersecurity competitions at the national level, going to Washington DC for free just because I liked computers! Really, it's amazing how far some tinkering can take you.
